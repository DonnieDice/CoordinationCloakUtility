CCU - Coordination Cloak Utility - One Click, Any Destination!

The smartest teleportation addon for World of Warcraft!

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(149, 0, 65)][B]What is CCU?[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
CCU automates the entire teleportation cloak process. Never again forget to re-equip your original cloak after teleporting! With one click, CCU handles detection, swapping, using, and restoring your cloaks automatically.

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(149, 0, 65)][B]Key Features[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
• [B]Smart Cloak Detection[/B] - Automatically finds all teleportation cloaks in your bags
• [B]One-Click Teleportation[/B] - Single button handles the entire process
• [B]Auto Gear Swap[/B] - Saves and restores your original cloak automatically
• [B]Combat Safe[/B] - Prevents gear changes during combat
• [B]Zero Configuration[/B] - Works immediately after installation

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(149, 0, 65)][B]How It Works[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
1. Type [CODE]/ccu[/CODE] to activate the addon
2. Click the teleport button that appears
3. CCU handles everything automatically:
   • Scans for teleportation cloaks
   • Saves your current cloak
   • Equips the teleport cloak
   • Uses the teleport ability
   • Re-equips your original cloak

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(149, 0, 65)][B]Supported Cloaks[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
• [B]Cloak of Coordination[/B] (Guild cloak - All versions)
• [B]Wrap of Unity[/B] (Alliance - All versions)
• [B]Shroud of Cooperation[/B] (Horde - All versions)
• All upgraded and variant versions

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(149, 0, 65)][B]Commands[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[CODE]/ccu[/CODE] - Activate addon and show teleport button
[CODE]/ccu help[/CODE] - Display all available commands
[CODE]/ccu welcome[/CODE] - Toggle the welcome message

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(149, 0, 65)][B]Advanced Features[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
• [B]Smart Button Positioning[/B] - Draggable and remembers location
• [B]Intelligent Management[/B] - Prioritizes highest item level cloak
• [B]Combat Awareness[/B] - Queues actions if in combat
• [B]Error Handling[/B] - Handles interrupted teleports gracefully
• [B]Cooldown Tracking[/B] - Shows exact time remaining

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(149, 0, 65)][B]Compatibility[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
• The War Within (110002+)
• Dragonflight (100207)
• All previous retail expansions

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(149, 0, 65)][B]Installation[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
1. Download CCU
2. Extract to your Interface/AddOns folder
3. Type /ccu to start using!

[SIZE=4][COLOR=rgb(149, 0, 65)][B]Troubleshooting[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[B]Button not appearing?[/B]
• Ensure you have a teleportation cloak in your bags
• Type /ccu to manually trigger
• Check addon is enabled

[B]Cloak not swapping?[/B]
• Verify you're not in combat
• Make sure cloak slot isn't locked
• Try /reload to refresh UI
