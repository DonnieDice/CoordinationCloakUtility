## <span style="color:#9b59b6">🪄 What is CCU?</span>

**<span style="color:#9b59b6">C</span><span style="color:#9b59b6">C</span><span style="color:#9b59b6">U</span> <span style="color:#3598db">|</span> <span style="color:#9b59b6">C</span><span style="color:#fff">oordination </span><span style="color:#9b59b6">C</span><span style="color:#fff">loak </span><span style="color:#9b59b6">U</span><span style="color:#fff">tility</span>** <span style="color:#e67e23">automates the tedious process of equipping, using, and re-equipping teleportation cloaks in</span> <span style="color:#06c">World of Warcraft</span><span style="color:#e67e23">. One click to scan your bags, swap your cloak, teleport, and restore your original gear — all automatically.</span>

### <span style="color:#9b59b6">🔥 Why Choose CCU?</span>
- **<span style="color:#2dc26b">🪄 Smart Detection:</span>** <span style="color:#e67e23">Automatically finds all teleportation cloaks in your bags</span>
- **<span style="color:#9b59b6">⚡ One-Click:</span>** <span style="color:#e67e23">Equip, use, and re-equip with a single button press</span>
- **<span style="color:#b96ad9">🛡️ Combat Safe:</span>** <span style="color:#e67e23">Prevents gear changes during combat</span>
- **<span style="color:#4ecdc4">🌍 10+ Languages:</span>** <span style="color:#e67e23">Supports all major WoW locales</span>
- **<span style="color:#ff6b6b">💬 Active Support:</span>** <span style="color:#e67e23">Join our Discord for instant help!</span>

## <span style="color:#9b59b6">🔧 Troubleshooting</span>

**<span style="color:#ff6b6b">Button not appearing?</span>**
- <span style="color:#e67e23">Ensure you have a teleportation cloak in your bags</span>
- <span style="color:#e67e23">Type</span> `/ccu` <span style="color:#e67e23">to manually trigger the addon</span>
- <span style="color:#e67e23">Check that the addon is enabled in your AddOns menu</span>

**<span style="color:#ff6b6b">Cloak not swapping?</span>**
- <span style="color:#e67e23">Verify you are not in combat — gear cannot change during combat</span>
- <span style="color:#e67e23">Make sure your cloak slot is not locked</span>
- <span style="color:#e67e23">Try</span> `/reload` <span style="color:#e67e23">to refresh the UI</span>

**<span style="color:#ff6b6b">Error messages?</span>**
- <span style="color:#e67e23">"No usable cloaks found" — all your teleport cloaks are on cooldown</span>
- <span style="color:#e67e23">"Cannot change gear in combat" — wait until combat ends</span>
- <span style="color:#e67e23">"Item info unavailable" — the addon is loading item data, try again momentarily</span>

**<span style="color:#ff6b6b">Still having trouble?</span>**
- <span style="color:#e67e23">Join our</span> [<span style="color:#7289da">Discord</span>](https://discord.gg/N7kdKAHVVF) <span style="color:#e67e23">for instant support</span>
